# Principles for the amendment of the ENS filings

1. Amendment of an ENS filings lodged via message IE3Axx replaces the previous version of the ENS filing lodged via message IE3Fxx.

2., ENS filing IE3F25 shall not be amended as this filing contains the information (Transport document (Master level)) that has to be unique and cannot be amended.
