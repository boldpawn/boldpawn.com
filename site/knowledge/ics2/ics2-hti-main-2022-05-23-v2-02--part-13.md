# Scope of the HTI specifications

ICS2 Harmonised Trader Interface Specifications cover:

- Lodgement and registration of the ENS filings (full or partial) and Arrival notifications via the Trader Interface;

- Exchanges of requests, responses and notifications between the economic operators and Customs administrations;

- Processing  of the referrals;

- Consultation of the ENS;

- Invalidation and amendment of ENS filings;

Not in scope of CFSS:

- Specifications for the customs declarations containing ENS particulars as per Art. 130 UCC;

- ENS filing for postal items on other modes of transport than air;

- ENS filing for express consignments on other modes of transport than air.
