## Notes for the answering agent

- **Diagrams (BPM L3.5 / L4):** the HLS*/L4* files are faithful text transcriptions of BPMN diagrams (swimlanes, events, tasks, gateways, sequence flow, and — for L4 — the IE messages). Image/arrow geometry is not preserved; the flow is written out in text.
- **IE files originate from the XLSX workspace** (richer than the RTF source: explicit Level column, full column set). In-cell line breaks are rendered as `<br>`.
- **Cross-references:** to fully resolve a message element, follow its `Rules`/`Conditions` codes to the R&C doc and its `ICS2 CL (MS)`/`ICS2 CL (Trade)` codes to the CL doc.
- **`xx` placeholders:** in the BPM/IE narrative, `IE3Fxx`/`IE3Axx` are generic references; the concrete messages are the IE3F**/IE3A** files in Catalog 4b/4c.
