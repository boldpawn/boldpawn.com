## Scope

**This document covers:**

- Specifications of the ICS2 Master process with underlying sub-processes to be implemented by the components of the ICS2 system: Trader Interface (TI), Common Repository (CR),

- Supporting processes which will offer additional functionalities to the Member States and the Trade, such as the consultation for specific ENS filings by the Trade.

**The document does not cover:**

- Specifications for the customs declarations containing ENS particulars as per Art. 130 UCC.
